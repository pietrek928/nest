## Conclusions

- **Proposition seeds**: small +0.4 parts vs `shipped_no_props` on average; keep `use_guidance_propositions=True`.
- **Grid exploration**: `guidance_enable_grid=False` wins (faster pipeline, highest parts_mean).
- **Squeeze**: `guidance_use_tight_packing=True` with weight 0.4; disabling squeeze or heavy squeeze (0.6) does not improve pipeline score.
- **max_propositions=3**: same as full props on this fixture; keep 5 for diversity headroom.

Shipped defaults updated in `ProposeConfig`: propositions on, grid off, tight packing on.
